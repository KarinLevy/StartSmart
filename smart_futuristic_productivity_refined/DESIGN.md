## Brand & Style
This design system is built to balance high-performance productivity with a calm, encouraging atmosphere. The aesthetic blends **Minimalism** with subtle **Glassmorphism** and **Soft Futuristic** elements. It is designed for professionals who need clarity and focus without sacrificing a modern, tech-forward feel.

## Colors
The palette is anchored by a **Deep Professional Blue** (#00236f) for stability and authority, complemented by a **Refined Electric Purple** (#6a36d2) which serves as a vibrant secondary accent for action and creativity. A **Deep Earthy Umber** (#4b1c00) acts as a tertiary grounding tone. The system uses a "fidelity" variant, ensuring that UI elements maintain high color accuracy.

## Elevation & Depth
Visual hierarchy is established using **Ambient Shadows** and **Tonal Layers**. Instead of harsh black shadows, we use soft, diffused shadows tinted with the primary blue to maintain a "glassy" appearance. Surface-container tiers create stacked depth for complex information layouts.

## Shapes
The shape language is defined by **large, friendly radiuses**. Standard components utilize a 0.5rem (8px) base radius, while high-level containers like task cards and dashboard widgets use a 24px (1.5rem) corner radius to create a soft, modern containerization of content.

## Typography
We utilize **Geist** across all roles to maintain a sleek, monolinear, and highly legible appearance. The scale prioritizes hierarchy through weight and tight letter-spacing for large displays. Mobile-specific headline sizes ensure that the futuristic feel remains accessible on all device form factors.

## Layout & Spacing
The system follows an **8px base unit** to ensure mathematical harmony. We employ a fluid-to-fixed hybrid model with 32px container padding and 24px gutters, providing generous breathing room to reduce cognitive load during high-productivity tasks.
